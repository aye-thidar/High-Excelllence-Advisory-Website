About Us

A right option for your brighter future

Our Story

High Excellence Advisory is a dynamic and licensed student recruitement agency based in the heart of Petaling Jaya, Selangor, Malaysia. We specialized in connecting aspiring international students from ASEAN regions with top-tier private colleges and universities in Malaysia.

With a deep understanding of Malaysian eduction landscape and internation student needs, our mission is to provide trasparent, ethical and high-quality advisory services to empower students with the right educational opportunities.

Our Vision

To be a trusted educational bridge between Malaysia and emerging student markets across ASEAN and Africa, fostering global learning, cultural exchange, and academic excellence.

Our Mission

• To assist international students in selecting the most suitable Malaysian college or university based on academic goals, career aspirations, and budget.

• To provide end-to-end student placement services including program selection, admission processing, visa guidance, and pre-departure support.

• To support Malaysian higher education institutions in expanding their reach and diversity by tapping into new and emerging student markets.
