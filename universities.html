Why Choose Malaysia?

Malaysia has become a preferred destination for international students due to:

Affordable tuition fees and cost of living
English as the medium of instruction
Multicultural society with high safety standards
Home to globally ranked institutions and twinning programs with UK, Australia, and the US
Strategic location at the heart of Southeast Asia
Easier visa processing and student-friendly immigration policies
Less restrictive entry and financial requirements compared to the US, Australia, and many European countries
Muslim friendly country
Diversity
